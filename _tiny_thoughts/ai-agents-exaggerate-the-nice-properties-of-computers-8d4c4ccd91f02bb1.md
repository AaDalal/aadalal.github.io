---
title: "ai agents exaggerate the \"nice\" properties of computers:"
date: 2026-05-21
---

ai agents exaggerate the "nice" properties of computers:
* determinism
* declarativeness
* simplicity and uniformity (the avoidance of special cases)
because they operate with the reasoning capabilities of humans but 1000x the output

In many cases the affordances we provide to humans can be provided by agents or created at a second level
